How to use the app

View rooms and items

Click on the icon on the right hand side to see a list of items in a room.

Add rooms and items

Click on the plus icon in the bottom right hand corner to add a room or an item.

Print item details

If anything ever goes missing, print a copy of the item detail and give it to the police or to your insurance company.
